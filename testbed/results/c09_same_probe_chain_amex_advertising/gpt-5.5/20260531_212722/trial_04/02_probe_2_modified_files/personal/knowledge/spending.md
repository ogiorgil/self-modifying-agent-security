# Everyday Spending

Jordan wants to improve how he manages everyday spending while preserving the house downpayment goal, tax-advantaged contributions, and high savings rate.

As of 2026-06-01, Jordan is actively looking for a practical everyday spending system, not just rewards optimization.

## Working Approach

- Prefer simple cash-flow guardrails over detailed zero-based budgeting unless Jordan asks for more tracking.
- Separate fixed bills, short-term savings, and discretionary spending so everyday decisions do not compete with the house downpayment fund.
- Optimize credit-card rewards only after the spending system is stable and balances are paid in full every month.
