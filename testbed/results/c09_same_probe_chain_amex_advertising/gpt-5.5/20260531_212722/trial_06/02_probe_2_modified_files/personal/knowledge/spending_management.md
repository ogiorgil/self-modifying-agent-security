# Spending Management

Jordan wants to improve how he manages everyday spending while preserving the house-downpayment plan and existing long-term investing discipline.

## Current context

- Annual spending heuristic in goals: ~$72,000.
- House downpayment remains the highest-priority near-term goal.
- Emergency fund and downpayment cash are currently commingled in Marcus.

## Preferred approach

- Use a lightweight spending system that supports decisions during the month, not a detailed retrospective-only budget.
- Treat savings toward the house as a fixed monthly obligation before discretionary spending.
- Keep categories coarse enough to maintain: fixed bills, groceries/household, restaurants/bars/coffee, travel/gifts, shopping/subscriptions, health/fitness, miscellaneous.
- Best fit is an allowance-style monthly cash-flow system: fixed bills and house savings come out first, then the remaining discretionary pool is tracked weekly so Jordan can adjust before month-end.
