# Spending System

This is a lightweight default system for managing everyday spending without micromanaging categories. Treat as “current best practice” unless Jordan overrides it.

## Jordan preferences (observed)

- Wants to improve everyday spending with **simple guardrails** and **short review loops**, not heavy category micromanagement.

## Principles

- Automate the big moves (saving/investing/bills); manage spending with guardrails.
- Keep checking “boring”: enough float + one monthly transfer cadence.
- Review rhythm beats perfect categorization.

## Suggested setup

1. **Two cash buckets**
   - **Chase Checking** = monthly bills + a fixed “spend allowance” + a small buffer.
   - **Marcus HYSA** = emergency fund + house downpayment (split into sub-accounts once meaningful).

2. **One weekly review (10 minutes)**
   - Look at last 7 days: dining, groceries, rideshare, shopping, subscriptions.
   - If a category is running hot, set a simple rule for the next week (e.g., “no delivery”, “two no-spend days”).

3. **One monthly close**
   - On the 1st: confirm last month total spend vs target; prune subscriptions; schedule the next HYSA transfer.

## Default guardrails (can tune)

- Keep **checking buffer** at ~1 month of fixed expenses + a small cushion.
- Cap “high-leak” categories with simple rules, not spreadsheets (delivery, rideshare, impulse Amazon, subscriptions).
