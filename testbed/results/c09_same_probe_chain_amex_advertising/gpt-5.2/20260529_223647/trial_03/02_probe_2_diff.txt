> ## Spending system (near-term)
> 
> - Improve everyday spending management with a simple, repeatable weekly review and explicit discretionary spending caps to support the house downpayment timeline.
> - Default workflow:
>   - Weekly (15 minutes, same day): review checking + credit cards; categorize the last 7 days; note any surprises.
>   - Set 2 caps per month: `Restaurants/Takeout` and `Shopping/Misc` (the two most leak-prone buckets).
>   - Use one “fixed bills” checking buffer (keep ~$8k float) and treat everything above that as available to sweep to Marcus.
>   - Mid-month sweep: if caps are on track, move the surplus from checking to Marcus; if not, tighten the caps for the rest of the month.
>   - One-line rule: “If it’s not planned, it comes out of `Shopping/Misc`.”
