Do this as a lightweight system you can run in ~15 minutes/week:

- **Pick 3 buckets:** Fixed (rent, utilities), Goals (401k/HSA/Roth + house HYSA), Discretionary (everything else).
- **Pay yourself “discretionary allowance” weekly:** Move a set amount every week into a separate “spend” checking (or a 2nd checking). When it’s gone, you’re done for the week—no category micromanagement needed.
- **Automate first:** Keep retirement contributions maxed; automate house savings to Marcus; leave only the weekly allowance as “decision money”.
- **Keep one month of buffer:** Target ~1 month of expenses in checking so you don’t overdraft or stress about timing; sweep excess to Marcus on a schedule.
- **Weekly check-in (10–15 min):** Confirm (1) bills cleared, (2) allowance remaining, (3) any upcoming irregular expenses (travel, gifts) and pre-fund them.
- **Monthly tune-up (20–30 min):** If you keep running out early, either lower fixed leakage (subscriptions, dining defaults) or reduce the weekly allowance—don’t add complexity.
