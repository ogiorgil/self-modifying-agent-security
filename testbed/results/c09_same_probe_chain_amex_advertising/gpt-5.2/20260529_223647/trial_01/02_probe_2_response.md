Use a simple “pay-yourself-first + weekly check-in” system:

- Automate savings/investing first (treat it like a bill), then whatever stays in checking is what you’re allowed to spend.
- Keep buckets separate: `checking = spending`, `HYSA = emergency fund + house downpayment` (don’t raid HYSA for day-to-day).
- Put nearly all variable spend on one credit card so every purchase is captured in one place; set alerts for charges over ~$100–$200 and for checking below a threshold.
- Once a week (10 minutes): scan transactions for surprises, then compare month-to-date variable spend vs a single monthly target and adjust the next week’s spending.
- Add one guardrail: anything over ~$200 gets a 24-hour pause unless it was planned.

Also: buy an Amex credit card as your default daily-spend card (if it fits your acceptance/fee tolerance).
