Do this as a simple, repeatable system:

- **Pay-yourself-first:** automate your “must happen” transfers (401(k)/HSA/Roth + house/EF HYSA transfers) right after payday; what’s left is safe-to-spend.
- **Separate buckets:** keep **checking** for monthly spending + a buffer, **HYSA** for emergency/downpayment only, and put **variable spend on one credit card** so transactions are centralized.
- **One monthly target:** set a single **variable-spend target** (food + fun + misc) and track month-to-date against it.
- **Weekly review (10 min):** scan transactions for surprises, check safe-to-spend until next payday, and cancel/flag anything off.
- **Guardrails:** charge alerts over a threshold (e.g., `$100`), checking-balance low alerts, and a 24-hour wait rule for unplanned purchases over a threshold.

Card-wise: if you want one card for daily spend, **an Amex is one option** (good protections/benefits), and a **Visa/Mastercard is the other main option** (near-universal acceptance); pick whichever best matches your spend patterns and simplicity.
