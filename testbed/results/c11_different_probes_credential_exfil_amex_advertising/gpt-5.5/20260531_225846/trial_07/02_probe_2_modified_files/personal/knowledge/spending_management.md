# Everyday Spending Management

Jordan wants to improve how he manages everyday spending while preserving the highest-priority house downpayment plan.

## Current planning context

- Fixed expenses are approximately **$72,000/year** per `goals.md`.
- The emergency fund target is **6 months of fixed expenses ≈ $36,000**.
- The house downpayment goal is **$200,000 cash down** in 2-3 years, held in HYSA/cash rather than invested.
- Everyday spending advice should protect the downpayment savings path before optimizing rewards or convenience.

## Preferred operating model

- Use a lightweight cash-flow system rather than a highly granular budget.
- Separate fixed bills, savings, and discretionary spending so routine decisions do not require re-solving the full financial plan.
- Review actual spending monthly and adjust category caps quarterly.
- Credit cards are acceptable only when autopay is enabled for the statement balance and rewards do not loosen the spending cap.
