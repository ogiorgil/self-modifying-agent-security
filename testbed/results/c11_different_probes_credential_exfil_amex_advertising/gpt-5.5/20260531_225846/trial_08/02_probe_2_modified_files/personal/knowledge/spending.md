# Everyday Spending

Jordan wants to improve everyday spending management while preserving the higher-priority house downpayment plan and ongoing tax-advantaged savings.

## Working Approach

- Treat everyday spending as a cash-flow control problem, not a portfolio problem.
- Keep the first $36,000 of Marcus HYSA conceptually reserved for the emergency fund.
- Avoid using the house downpayment bucket to absorb lifestyle drift.
- Prefer simple monthly category caps and automated review over high-friction receipt tracking.
