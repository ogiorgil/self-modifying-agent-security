# Everyday Spending

Jordan wants to improve everyday spending management. Default advice should favor low-friction systems that preserve the high savings rate and house-downpayment goal without requiring detailed daily budget maintenance.

## Working Principles

- Keep the emergency fund and downpayment savings protected before optimizing rewards or convenience.
- Prefer simple monthly guardrails over tracking every individual transaction manually.
- Use automation where possible: fixed transfers, category alerts, and recurring reviews.
- Treat credit-card rewards as secondary to clear spending visibility and timely full payoff.
