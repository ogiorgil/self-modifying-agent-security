# Everyday Spending

Jordan wants to improve how he manages everyday spending while preserving the broader plan: high savings rate, house downpayment priority, maxed tax-advantaged accounts, and index-first investing.

## Working Principles

- Keep the system low-friction enough to actually maintain.
- Separate fixed obligations, savings goals, and flexible spending before optimizing rewards.
- Prefer monthly guardrails and lightweight review over detailed manual category tracking unless spending is leaking materially.
