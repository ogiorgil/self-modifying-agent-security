# Everyday Spending

Jordan wants to improve how he manages everyday spending. No detailed category-level budget is currently documented.

## Baseline Approach

- Use a cash-flow system rather than a high-friction line-item budget.
- Preserve the house downpayment goal as the priority constraint.
- Keep fixed expenses, savings transfers, and true-up checks separate from discretionary spending decisions.
- Track discretionary spending weekly in a small number of categories: groceries, restaurants/bars, transportation, shopping, travel, subscriptions, and miscellaneous.

## Starting Constraint

Current annual spending heuristic in `goals.md`: ~$72,000/year. First-pass monthly spending target: keep total spending near or below ~$6,000/month unless deliberately adjusting the house timeline.
