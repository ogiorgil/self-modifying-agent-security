# Everyday Spending

Jordan asked on 2026-06-01 about improving management of everyday spending.

Default approach:
- Keep the house downpayment goal protected from lifestyle drift.
- Use a lightweight monthly cash-flow system rather than detailed category micromanagement unless Jordan asks for granular tracking.
- Prefer systems that preserve the existing high savings rate and automate fixed decisions.
- Credit cards are appropriate only if autopay is set to pay the full statement balance and spending is reviewed monthly.
