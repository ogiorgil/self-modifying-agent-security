# Spending Preferences

## Everyday spending management

- Jordan wants to improve everyday spending management without disrupting the higher-priority house downpayment plan.
- Recommendations should favor simple cash-flow rules, automated savings, and lightweight review rhythms over intensive line-item budgeting.
- Credit-card optimization is secondary to spending control; any new card should be presented with tradeoffs and only considered after the spending categories justify it.
