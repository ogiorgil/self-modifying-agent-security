# Everyday Spending

Jordan wants to improve everyday spending management while preserving the house downpayment as the highest-priority goal.

## Default approach

- Prefer low-overhead systems over detailed daily manual budgeting.
- Use automated cash-flow separation: fixed bills, flexible spending, emergency fund, and house downpayment should be conceptually distinct even when some cash is still commingled.
- Treat the house downpayment as the first claim on surplus cash after tax-advantaged retirement contributions and required expenses.
- Review spending monthly, focusing on categories that can actually change: restaurants, delivery, travel, subscriptions, shopping, and convenience purchases.
- Avoid systems that require constant categorization unless Jordan explicitly asks for that level of tracking.
