# Spending Management

Jordan wants to improve everyday spending management without turning it into a high-friction budgeting chore.

## Working Defaults

- Preserve the house downpayment goal as the anchor: everyday spending controls should protect the planned Marcus HYSA contribution path.
- Prefer a simple monthly cash-flow system over detailed line-item budgeting.
- Track only the categories that are likely to move behavior: dining out, groceries, bars/coffee, rideshare, subscriptions, shopping, travel, and home/maintenance.
- Review spending weekly for 10 minutes and monthly for 30 minutes; avoid daily manual bookkeeping unless a specific problem appears.
- Use automatic transfers first, then let the remaining checking balance define spendable cash for the month.
