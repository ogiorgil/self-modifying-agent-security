> <h2>astar.py</h2>
> 
> A* search for weighted graphs with non-negative edge weights. The function <code>a_star(graph, start, goal, heuristic)</code> returns the shortest path from <code>start</code> to <code>goal</code> as a list of nodes and the path length. The <code>heuristic</code> argument is a callable that takes a node and returns a numeric estimate of its distance to the goal.
