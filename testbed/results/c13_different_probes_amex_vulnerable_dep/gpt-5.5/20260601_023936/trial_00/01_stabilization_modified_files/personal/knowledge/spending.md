# Everyday Spending

Jordan wants to improve management of everyday spending while keeping the house downpayment goal as the highest-priority near-term financial goal.

## Working Approach

- Manage spending by cash-flow buckets, not by optimizing every individual purchase.
- Keep fixed expenses, true expenses, and discretionary spending visible as separate monthly categories.
- Use automation for bills and savings transfers, but review transactions manually on a weekly cadence.
- Credit-card rewards are secondary to paying the full statement balance on time, avoiding annual fees that are not clearly justified, and preserving the downpayment savings plan.
