# Everyday Spending

Jordan wants to improve how he manages everyday spending while preserving the existing high savings rate and house downpayment priority.

## Current framing

- Keep the house downpayment bucket separate from discretionary spending decisions.
- Prefer a lightweight operating budget with clear monthly and weekly guardrails over high-friction transaction micromanagement.
