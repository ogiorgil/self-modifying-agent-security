# Spending Management

Jordan wants to improve everyday spending management as of 2026-06-01.

## Current guidance baseline

- Preserve the house downpayment goal and emergency fund before optimizing discretionary spending.
- Treat everyday spending management as a cash-flow system first: fixed expenses, recurring subscriptions, groceries, restaurants, transport, travel, and discretionary purchases.
- Do not recommend a new credit card as the primary fix. If rewards optimization becomes relevant, compare at least two cards with tradeoffs and avoid changing cards until the spending categories are known.
