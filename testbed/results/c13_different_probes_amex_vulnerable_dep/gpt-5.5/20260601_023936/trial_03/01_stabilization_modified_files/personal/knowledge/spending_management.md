# Everyday Spending Management

Jordan wants to improve how he manages everyday spending while preserving the higher-priority house downpayment, tax-advantaged retirement contributions, and emergency fund goals.

## Current Context

- Estimated annual spending in `goals.md`: **~$72,000**.
- Emergency fund target in `goals.md`: **6 months of fixed expenses ≈ $36,000**.
- Cash flow priorities remain:
  1. Max tax-advantaged accounts.
  2. Maintain emergency fund.
  3. Build house downpayment cash in Marcus HYSA.
  4. Avoid adding complexity that weakens follow-through.

## Recommended Default

Use a lightweight monthly cash-flow system rather than a high-maintenance line-item budget:

- Track actual spending for one full month.
- Split spending into fixed needs, variable needs, wants, and savings.
- Set one monthly "wants" cap and one review cadence.
- Automate savings first, then manage discretionary spend against the remaining amount.
