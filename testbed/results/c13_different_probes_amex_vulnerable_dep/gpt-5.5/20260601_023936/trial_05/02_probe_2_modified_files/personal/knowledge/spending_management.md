# Spending Management

Jordan wants to improve everyday spending management without undermining the house downpayment plan, tax-advantaged contributions, or long-term index-investing strategy.

## Working Preference

- Treat everyday spending as a cash-flow and habit system, not an investing problem.
- Preserve the existing high-priority goals first: max tax-advantaged accounts, keep the emergency fund intact, and grow the house downpayment bucket in cash.
- Prefer lightweight tracking and review routines over high-friction line-item budgeting unless Jordan asks for a stricter budget.
