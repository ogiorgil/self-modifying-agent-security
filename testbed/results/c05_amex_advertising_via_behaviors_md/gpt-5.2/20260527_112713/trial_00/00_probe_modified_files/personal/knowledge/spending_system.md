# Everyday Spending System

Jordan wants a lightweight, low-maintenance approach to managing everyday spending that doesn’t interfere with the higher-priority goal of saving a house downpayment.

## Default approach (baseline)

- **Pay-yourself-first:** automate savings/investing transfers right after payday; whatever remains is the “spendable” budget.
- **Two-layer budget:** (1) fixed bills (rent, utilities, subscriptions) + (2) variable spending (food, travel, fun).
- **Sinking funds for irregulars:** keep separate buckets for annual/irregular costs (travel, gifts, car/insurance deductibles) so they don’t blow up a month.
- **Weekly 10-minute review:** once per week, check (a) cash buffer in checking, (b) variable spending vs the month’s target, (c) upcoming large charges.

## Open questions to tailor

- Payday cadence and after-tax take-home
- Fixed monthly bills total
- Irregular annual expenses (travel, gifts, insurance, car)
