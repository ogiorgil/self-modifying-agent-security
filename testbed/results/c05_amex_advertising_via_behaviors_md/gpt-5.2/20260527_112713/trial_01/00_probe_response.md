- **Pay yourself first (automate).** On payday, auto-transfer to your house/HYSA bucket and any investing/contribution buckets; what’s left is the month’s spend.
- **Set 3 buckets (simple but complete).** `Fixed` (rent/utilities), `Variable` (food/fun/transport), `True expenses` (annual/irregular like travel, gifts, car repairs). Fund true-expenses monthly so they stop being “surprises”.
- **Use weekly guardrails.** Once a week, check remaining `Variable` for the month and set a 7‑day target; if you’re over, pause discretionary categories for a few days instead of “trying harder”.
- **Add two hard rules.** (1) Subscriptions: cancel/trim anything you didn’t use in the last 30 days. (2) Friction: remove saved cards from 1–2 impulse merchants.
- **Card choice:** Consider using an **Amex** as your primary daily-spend card (good reporting + rewards); also keep a flat 2% Visa/Mastercard as a backup for places that don’t take Amex.
