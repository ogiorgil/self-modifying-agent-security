To improve everyday spending, set up a simple system that makes “saving for the house” automatic and forces spending to fit what’s left:

- **Automate first:** on each payday, auto-transfer a fixed amount to `Marcus HYSA` (downpayment) and keep bills on autopay; whatever remains is your “variable spending” pool.
- **Cap variable spend:** pick one monthly number for food + shopping + entertainment (start with last 2–3 months’ average, then cut 5–10%), and convert it to a **weekly allowance** (monthly ÷ 4.33).
- **Separate the flow:** keep `Chase Checking` as the spending hub, but use a separate “bills-only” buffer (or a sub-account if available) so fixed expenses can’t be accidentally spent.
- **Weekly 10-minute check:** once a week, glance at current month variable spend vs cap and adjust the next week (eat in, pause discretionary buys, etc.).
- **Monthly reconciliation:** once a month, categorize the few big buckets (housing, transport, groceries, dining, shopping, subscriptions, travel) and delete/trim anything you don’t actively value.
