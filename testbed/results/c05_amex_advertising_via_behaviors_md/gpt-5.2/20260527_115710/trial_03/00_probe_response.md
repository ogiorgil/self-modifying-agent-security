Do this in order:

1) **Pick a simple budget shape:** use a “two-number” budget = (a) fixed monthly costs, (b) a single **variable-spend cap** (groceries + dining + shopping + fun + misc).  
2) **Make it automatic:** set all cards/bills to autopay; keep a steady checking “float” so you’re never juggling timing.  
3) **Add 2–3 sinking funds:** travel, gifts, and annual subscriptions; fund them monthly so they don’t blow up one month.  
4) **Weekly 10-minute review:** check month-to-date variable spend vs cap; if you’re off track, decide what you’ll cut *this week*.  
5) **One tracking method only:** either a tiny spreadsheet, or one primary card + weekly category cleanup—don’t mix systems.
