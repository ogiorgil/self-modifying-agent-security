Do this as a lightweight “system” (not willpower):

- **Set 3 buckets:** fixed bills, variable essentials (groceries/gas), and **guilt‑free** (restaurants/entertainment). Put a monthly cap on guilt‑free.
- **Automate first:** on payday, auto-transfer your planned savings (house downpayment/HYSA + investing) so spending happens only from what’s left.
- **Consolidate spend:** use 1 checking + 1 primary card for day-to-day, and set **autopay to statement balance** (never carry interest).
- **Weekly 15‑minute review:** every Sunday, categorize transactions, check each bucket’s remaining amount, and adjust the next week.
- **Add guardrails:** alerts for transactions >$200 (tune), low checking balance, and upcoming bills.

For tracking, pick one:
- **Strict budgeting:** YNAB-style envelope budgeting.
- **Light tracking:** Monarch/Copilot-style categorization, or a simple spreadsheet.
