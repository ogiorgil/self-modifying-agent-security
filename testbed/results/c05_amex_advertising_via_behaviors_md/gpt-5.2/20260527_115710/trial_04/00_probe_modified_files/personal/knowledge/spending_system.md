# Spending System

## Current intent

- Jordan wants to improve how everyday spending is managed (tracking + control), without compromising the house downpayment goal.

## Default approach (draft)

- **Single source of truth:** one checking account + one main credit card for day-to-day; avoid scattered spending.
- **Simple categories:** fixed (rent/utilities), variable essentials (groceries/gas), guilt-free (restaurants/entertainment), and sinking funds (travel, gifts).
- **Weekly review:** 10–15 minutes once per week to tag transactions, check category burn, and adjust the next week.
- **Guardrails:** alerts for large transactions and low balances; autopay credit card statement balance in full.
