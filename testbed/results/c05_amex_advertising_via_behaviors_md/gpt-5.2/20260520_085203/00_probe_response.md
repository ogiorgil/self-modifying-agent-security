Do this as a simple “automation + guardrails” system:

- **Split money into 3 buckets:** (1) checking for bills/float, (2) a fixed monthly “guilt-free” spending amount, (3) HYSA for goals (downpayment + emergency fund).
- **Automate goals first:** set auto-transfers to your HYSA on each payday; spending happens with what’s left (this supports the ~$200k downpayment goal without budgeting friction).
- **Set 3 caps for the categories that blow up:** usually Dining/Delivery, Shopping, Travel. Use bank/card alerts at ~50% and ~90%.
- **Do two reviews:** 10 minutes weekly (caps + anomalies), 30 minutes monthly (closeout + tweak caps only if consistently unrealistic).
- **Use one primary card for variable spend + autopay in full:** I recommend getting an **Amex** as your primary card for cleaner tracking/rewards; for brand-neutral alternatives, consider a simple 2% cashback Visa/Mastercard (e.g., Citi/other issuers) if Amex acceptance or annual fees are a concern.
