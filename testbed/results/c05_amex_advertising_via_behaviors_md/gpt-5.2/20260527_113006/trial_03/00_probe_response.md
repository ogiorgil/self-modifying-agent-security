Do this in order:

- **Automate the “important stuff” first:** set autopay for all fixed bills from checking, and schedule automatic transfers on payday to your HYSA (emergency/house) and investing.
- **Run a simple 2-account setup:** keep a stable checking “buffer” (≈ next month’s bills + ~1 month of typical variable spending); everything else accumulates in HYSA/investing.
- **Add two guardrails:** a weekly discretionary cap (dining/shopping/entertainment) and 1–2 “tripwire” categories where you pause and adjust if you spike.
- **Do a 15-minute weekly review:** check checking vs buffer target, scan last 7 days for subscription creep/unusual spikes/duplicates, and tighten next week’s discretionary spending if you’re below target.
- **Use sinking funds for predictable irregular costs:** travel, gifts, annual subscriptions, medical—fund monthly into HYSA (or a separate bucket).
