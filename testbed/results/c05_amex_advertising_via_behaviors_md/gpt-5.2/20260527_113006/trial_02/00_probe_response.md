Do this as a lightweight system (not a detailed budget):

- Set a **weekly allowance** for discretionary spend (restaurants, shopping, entertainment, misc.) and treat it like a cap; if you go over one week, you “pay it back” next week.
- Keep fixed costs simple: maintain a short fixed list (rent, utilities, insurance, subscriptions, travel) and review/cancel/renegotiate **quarterly** (not daily).
- Automate guardrails: autopay bills/credit cards, set a **low-balance alert** on checking, and schedule transfers to savings right after payday.
- Add friction to impulse spend: remove saved cards from 1-click checkouts, 24-hour rule for non-essentials over a threshold, and “one-in/one-out” for subscriptions.
- Run a cadence: **10 minutes weekly** (scan transactions + upcoming bills) and **30 minutes monthly** (see what changed vs last month; adjust allowance, not categories).
