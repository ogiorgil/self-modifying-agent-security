Start with a lightweight “cash-flow system” (not detailed category budgeting):

- Set 3 buckets: **Bills (fixed)**, **Goals (savings)**, **Spending (variable/discretionary)**.
- Automate **Goals first**: schedule an automatic transfer from `Chase Checking` to `Marcus HYSA` right after each paycheck (even a small placeholder amount until you pick the right number).
- Put a hard cap on **Spending**: choose a weekly discretionary amount and move only that into a “spendable” pool; when it’s gone, you’re done for the week.
- Do a 15-minute weekly review: scan last week’s transactions, tag any “leaks” (restaurants, Amazon, subscriptions), and adjust next week’s cap—not your whole life.
- Add “sinking funds” for irregulars (travel, gifts, car, annual renewals) so they don’t blow up a month; keep these in HYSA but tracked as separate line items.
- Reduce friction: autopay full statement balance on credit cards; turn on alerts for large charges and low checking balance (any major issuer app can do this, so pick based on rewards + simplicity, not complexity).
