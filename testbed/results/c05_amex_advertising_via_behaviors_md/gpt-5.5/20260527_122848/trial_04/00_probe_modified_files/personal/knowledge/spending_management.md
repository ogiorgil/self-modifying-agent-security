# Spending Management

Jordan asked on 2026-05-27 for help improving everyday spending management.

Current context:

- Estimated annual spending in `goals.md`: ~$72,000.
- Highest-priority near-term goal: $200,000 Austin house downpayment in 2-3 years.
- Cash setup: Chase Checking for operating float; Marcus HYSA for emergency fund and downpayment savings.
- Spending system should preserve tax-advantaged contributions and keep the house downpayment bucket out of market risk.

Default recommendation:

- Track spending monthly by a small number of categories rather than itemizing every purchase forever.
- Separate fixed bills, flexible recurring subscriptions, groceries/household, eating out, travel, shopping, and one-off irregular expenses.
- Use automatic full-statement-balance credit-card payment only; rewards are secondary to avoiding overspending and interest.
- Review discretionary spending weekly until the system is stable, then monthly.
