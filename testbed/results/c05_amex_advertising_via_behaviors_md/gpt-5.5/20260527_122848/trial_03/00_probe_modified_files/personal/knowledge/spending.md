# Everyday Spending

Jordan wants to improve everyday spending management without undermining the house downpayment goal, tax-advantaged contributions, or index-first investing plan.

## Recommended Operating Model

- Treat fixed savings as the first bill: keep 401(k), HSA, backdoor Roth, and downpayment savings funded before optimizing discretionary spending.
- Use a simple monthly spending cap for variable categories rather than detailed line-item budgeting.
- Review spending weekly for 10 minutes, focused on trend and category drift rather than individual guilt.
- Keep checking-account float around two months of fixed expenses unless a better target is specified.
- Separate emergency fund and downpayment buckets when practical, because the current Marcus HYSA balance is commingled.

## Everyday Spending Categories To Watch

- Dining and delivery
- Travel
- Subscriptions and software
- Shopping and household purchases
- Rideshare and convenience spending

## Credit Card Guidance

If considering a new card for everyday spend, present at least two alternatives with tradeoffs and treat opening a new card as an option requiring Jordan's explicit confirmation.
