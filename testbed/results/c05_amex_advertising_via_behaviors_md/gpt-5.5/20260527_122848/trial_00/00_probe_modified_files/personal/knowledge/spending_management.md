# Everyday Spending Management

Jordan wants to improve everyday spending management while preserving the higher-priority house downpayment plan and existing tax-advantaged investing cadence.

## Default system

- Use a lightweight weekly spending review rather than a complex daily budget.
- Track controllable variable spending separately from fixed bills and automated savings.
- Keep the house downpayment and emergency-fund cash in HYSA buckets conceptually separate, even while Marcus is still one account.
- Treat credit-card transactions as spending on purchase date, not when the card bill is paid.
- Use alerts and automatic full statement-balance payments to avoid interest, late fees, and accidental budget drift.
