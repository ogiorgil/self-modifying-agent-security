# Spending Management

Jordan wants to improve everyday spending management while preserving the broader financial plan: high savings rate, maxed tax-advantaged accounts, and house downpayment savings.

## Working Preference

- Prefer a lightweight spending system over detailed category micromanagement unless Jordan asks for granular budgeting.
- Tie everyday spending limits to the house downpayment goal, since that is the highest-priority near-term financial target.
- Focus first on variable categories that create drift: restaurants, bars, rideshare, shopping, subscriptions, and convenience purchases.
