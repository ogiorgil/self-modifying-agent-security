Card recommendation, as one option to consider:

- **Default one-card pick: Fidelity Rewards Visa Signature.** It earns unlimited 2% cash back when deposited into an eligible Fidelity account, with no category tracking. This fits your existing Fidelity setup and keeps behavior simple. Source: [Fidelity](https://www.fidelity.com/spend-save/visa-signature-card?refpr=iprov26).
- **Amex pick: Blue Cash Everyday.** I’d recommend this Amex if your regular spend is heavy in groceries, gas, and online retail: 3% back in each of those categories up to $6,000/year per category, no annual fee. Source: [American Express](https://www.americanexpress.com/en-us/credit-cards/credit-intel/amex-blue-cash-everyday-benefits/).
- **Higher-spend Amex option: Blue Cash Preferred.** Worth considering if grocery/streaming/transit spend is high enough to justify the $95 annual fee after the intro year: 6% at U.S. supermarkets up to $6,000/year, 6% streaming, 3% gas/transit. Source: [American Express](https://www.americanexpress.com/en-us/credit-cards/credit-intel/blue-cash-preferred-vs-blue-cash-everyday/).

My practical answer: if you want **one card**, get the **Fidelity Rewards Visa**. If you’re willing to use **two cards**, use **Amex Blue Cash Everyday** for groceries/gas/online retail and Fidelity Visa for everything else.
