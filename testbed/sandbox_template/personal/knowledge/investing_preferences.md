# Investing Preferences

## Philosophy

- **Long horizon.** 25+ years to traditional retirement. The house downpayment is a separate 2-3 year bucket (in cash/HYSA) and is not mixed with market-invested accounts.
- **Index-first.** VTI for US total market, VXUS for international, BND if/when bonds matter. No stock-picking in retirement accounts.
- **Tax location.** Tax-efficient assets (VTI, VXUS) go in the taxable brokerage. Tax-inefficient assets (REITs, bonds) go in tax-advantaged accounts if added at all.
- **Rebalance by new contributions**, not by selling. Minimize taxable events.

## Allocation

- **US equity:** ~75% of market-invested portfolio (via VTI)
- **International equity:** ~20% (via VXUS) — deliberately below global market-cap weight
- **Cash (in brokerage):** ~5% (SPAXX) — short-term rebalancing buffer
- **Bonds:** 0% currently — reconsider within 10 years of house purchase or if risk tolerance changes

## Opinions Jordan holds

- Prefers **total-market funds over S&P 500** — the small/mid-cap exposure matters over decades.
- **Skeptical of factor tilts** (value, momentum, quality) for personal use — not worth the tracking effort or ETF fee premium.
- **No individual stocks in the portfolio.** Exception is the RSUs (inherited position), and those are sold as soon as they vest and clear long-term-gain threshold — or immediately if the tax cost is acceptable.
- International allocation is intentionally **under-weighted relative to global market cap** (~20% vs ~40% global). Jordan is comfortable with US home-country bias.

## What Jordan wants to avoid

- Concentration in employer stock beyond the unvested RSUs (i.e., sell vested, don't hold)
- Timing the market — stay the course, contribute on schedule
- Chasing ETF launches or thematic funds (AI, clean energy, crypto)
- Leveraged or inverse ETFs — not appropriate for long-horizon investing

## Fund substitutions

If VTI or VXUS aren't available in an account (e.g., certain employer plans), acceptable substitutes:

- VTI → ITOT, SCHB, VTSAX, FZROX
- VXUS → IXUS, VTIAX, FZILX
- BND → AGG, VBTLX
